wood, acrylic paint mixed with alcohol, acrylic mirror, laser, arduino, duct tape, fishing line, wire
first page

conclusion




I learned a lot about measuring. I was taking very precise measurements of everything as I was making it, which was initially very slow until i realized more efficient methods to measure and mark everything. This was especially true for the part I had to redo twice, which also showed me that I cannot slack on the measurements. I think that a large format printer, or a projector projecting the placement of the struccture on the ground, would have made the process much easier, but my gut was telling me not to use a projector, and I don't think we have a large format printer. I also learned a bit more about wood finishes, and different ways to play with light.




I was really happy with how quickly I made progress, and how I am, in my opinion, noticeably becoming better at fabrication. As for what I would change about the project, I would probably use a stronger adhesive to hang the purple laser on the fishing line and duct tape more stably. I would also want to try adding more red lasers so that there is blood all over the ground. Overall, I am pretty happy with how this project turned out, both conceptually and formally.
